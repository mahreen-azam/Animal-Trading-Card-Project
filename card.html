Killer Whale

The orca (killer whale) is the largest dolphin.

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Average Length: 16ft - 26ft
  • Average Lifespan: 17 years - 29 years
  • Habitat: Cold Ocean Waters

The killer whale, also known as an orca, is a toothed whale. Killer whales have a diverse diet, although individual populations often specialize in particular types of prey. Some feed exclusively on fish, while others hunt marine mammals such as seals and other species of dolphin.
